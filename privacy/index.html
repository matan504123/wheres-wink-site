Privacy Policy

Where's Wink?

Home · Support

The short version

We collect nothing.

The slightly longer version

  • Where's Wink? has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs.
  • Your progress is stored only on your device (and in your personal device backups, which we cannot access).
  • The app makes no network connections except those Apple performs on every app's behalf for App Store purchases.
  • Purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details or identity.
  • There is no data to sell, share, or lose — because none is collected.

Children

The game is suitable for all ages. Because no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children either.
