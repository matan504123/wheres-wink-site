The story

Every night, Wink — the Sandman's small apprentice — delivers sleep to the town of Pillowbrook. Tonight his satchel of dream-sand tore open, and Wink tumbled into the townsfolk's dreams. The loose sand made every dream overgrow, and dreams that lose their color become nightmares. Dive into one dream each night, find Wink and the things that don't belong, and pour the color back.

Sixteen hand-crafted dreams. Thousands of drawings in every scene — a town drowning in bread, a sea hung upside-down in the sky, a fairground built from memories.
Turn the world to find its secrets. Some things only reveal themselves when you look at a dream sideways. Rotate freely — clouds become cats, waves become horses.
Color returns as you find. Every discovery pours color back into the faded dream, until the whole scene blooms.
Hints that never punish. A free, gentle hint ladder — a firefly, a glow, a reveal. No ads, no timers, no energy. Ever.
Play in your language. Every dream and clue reads naturally in English, Suomi, or עברית — with full right-to-left support — and switching is one tap in Settings.
Your progress follows you. Play on your iPhone, pick up on your iPad — your dreams and settings carry across your devices through your own iCloud. No account to make; new device or fresh install, it's all just there.
One fair price. The first dream is free. One small purchase wakes the whole town — everything included, forever, shareable with your family.
Private by design. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. We collect nothing.
