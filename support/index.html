Frequently asked

I bought the unlock on another device — how do I restore it?
Open Settings inside the game and tap “Restore purchases”. The unlock is tied to your Apple Account and is shareable with Family Sharing.
I'm stuck on a clue.
Tap the lightbulb. Hints are free and escalate gently: first a firefly drifts toward the area, then the region glows, then the spot is revealed. There is no penalty, ever.
What are “sideways secrets”?
Each dream hides two things that only make sense when the world is turned. Twist with two fingers (or tap the rotate button) and look again.
How do I unlock the secret Sheep's Dream?
Find all 60 lost sheep hidden across the dreams. The flock will take it from there.
Can I play in another language?
Yes — open Settings and choose English, Suomi, or עברית. Hebrew is laid out fully right-to-left. By default the game follows your device language.
The colors are hard on my eyes at night.
Settings offers Sepia and Night comfort modes for every dream.
